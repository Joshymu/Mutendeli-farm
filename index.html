Welcome to Mutenderi Integrated Farm

About Us

Mutenderi Integrated Farm Ltd. is a dynamic agricultural enterprise focused on producing premium organic crops using sustainable practices and environmentally friendly technology. We cultivate macadamia, avocado, eucalyptus, and legumes across strategic locations: Mutenderi farm in Ngoma District (50ha), Nyarubuye Sector in Kirehe District (10ha), and Karama Karangazi sector in Nyagatare District (10ha). Our crop selection is guided by market demand, profitability, and climate-resilient innovations, ensuring optimal yield and environmental stewardship, with prospects to expand to the Eastern region.

Vision

We aspire to be a catalyst for positive change, inspiring others to embrace sustainable living and environmental stewardship. We envision a greener and healthier planet for generations to come.

Mission

Our aim is to promote environmental sustainability by integrating responsible land use and farming practices. We aim to produce premium organic products while prioritizing animal welfare and sustainable land management.
